# PlanEx Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 11, 2026

## Summary

PlanEx does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server. Everything the app knows about you — your preferences, your favorites, your approximate location, your interaction history — lives only on your device, in the app's private storage. There is no backend, no user account, no third-party analytics, and no advertising.

## What data the app uses and where it's stored

| Data | Where it's stored | Leaves the device? |
|---|---|---|
| Preferences (interests, usual companionship, budget, accessibility) | App's private storage (DataStore) | No |
| Favorites | App's local database (Room) | No |
| Interaction history and personalized learning | App's private storage (DataStore) | No |
| Approximate location (optional) | Used in memory to calculate distances; never saved or sent anywhere | No |

## Network connections the app does make

PlanEx only connects to the internet for two things, neither of which includes personally identifiable data:

- Downloading your region's experience catalog (beaches, monuments, museums, trails...), published as static files on GitHub. It's an anonymous download, just like opening a public web page.
- - Checking the current weather on Open-Meteo for your approximate location (if you've granted the location permission) or for the center of the downloaded region (if you haven't). Only the coordinates needed for the weather query are sent; no user identifier is sent because the app has no accounts.
 
  - Neither of these connections includes your name, your preferences, your favorites, or your interaction history.
 
  - ## Permissions
 
  - - **Approximate location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION):** optional. If you don't grant it, the app still works using the region's center as a reference.
    - - **Internet:** needed for the two connections described above.
     
      - PlanEx does not request camera, microphone, contacts, external storage, or any other sensitive permission.
     
      - ## Deleting your data
     
      - You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Privacy → Delete all my data. This removes your preferences, favorites, statistics, and personalized learning, leaving the app as if freshly installed. Uninstalling the app automatically deletes anything that remained.
     
      - ## Children
     
      - PlanEx is not specifically directed at children and does not collect data from any user, regardless of age, beyond what's described above (which never leaves the device).
     
      - ## Changes to this policy
     
      - If this policy changes, the "last updated" date will be updated and, if the change is significant, it will also be reflected in the app's Google Play listing.
